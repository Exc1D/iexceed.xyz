About EXXEED

I used to be a PMA cadet (Philippine Military Academy). I never thought that I'd be coding my own website because it's far from the military life I pictured from 2021. I used to march wearing a dignified full dress grey, a pair of bilingly shiny shoes with a spotless rifle on my shoulders. Now, I am here grinding codes and making a digital universe of my own.

I guess life is not about what you want, it's about what you make of it. That is why I made Exceed, my developer persona. Here in my own tiny world, everything is possible. I can do all things through HIM.
